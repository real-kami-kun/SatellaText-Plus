What is SatellaText?

SatellaText is the official teletext service of Satellaview+, and completes the triangle of the Satellaview+ family of services. SatellaText (or SatTXT, for short) features many pages of information, including schedules, news, trivia, articles, and even games! And SatTXT is open and free for anyone to check out - all you need is a way to view internet teletext pages.
